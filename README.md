# 🎓 Student Performance Prediction Competition

Welcome to my machine learning competition! Can you build the best model to predict whether a student will Pass (1) or Fail (0)?

### 🏆 View the Live Leaderboard here: https://chaitanyameher.github.io/student-performance-ml/

### 🚀 How to Participate:
1. **Download the Data:** Download `train.csv` to train your model, and `test.csv` to make your predictions.
2. **Fork this Repository:** Click the "Fork" button at the top right of this page.
3. **Add your Submission:** - Generate your predictions and save them in a file named `submission.csv`. It should have two columns: `id` and `result`.
   - Upload your `submission.csv` into the **`submission/`** folder of your forked repository (replace the dummy file).
4. **Make a Pull Request:** Open a Pull Request (PR) from your fork back to my repository.
5. **Get Graded!** My automated GitHub Actions bot will instantly grade your file, comment your F1-Score on your PR, and update the live leaderboard!
